ChemVault Core is the main website and research platform of the ChemVault ecosystem.

It is designed to become a structured chemistry platform containing molecules, reagents, reactions, laboratory notes, and scientific records.

## Main goals

ChemVault Core aims to provide:

- a chemistry dashboard
- molecule passport pages
- reagent library pages
- reaction mechanism pages
- lab note archives
- literature and project records

## Planned modules

The planned modules include:

- **Dashboard** — overview of records and recent updates.
- **Molecules** — structured information about chemical compounds.
- **Reagents** — reagent passports with reaction scope and safety.
- **Reactions** — reaction mechanisms, conditions and exam traps.
- **Lab Notes** — undergraduate chemistry experiment records.
- **Literature** — reading notes and research references.

## Development direction

ChemVault Core should not only be a visual website. It should become a searchable and structured chemistry knowledge platform.
