ChemVault is presented as a commercial SaaS workspace for chemistry operations. The public site should help buyers understand the product, the operational problem it solves and the controls that make it credible.

## Positioning

ChemVault is for teams that need to coordinate chemistry work without losing track of ownership, approvals, files, permissions or status.

The product story is intentionally simple:

- **One workspace** for requests, workflows, approvals, assets and activity.
- **Controlled access** so roles and permissions are understandable before a team scales.
- **Operational visibility** so managers can see progress, blockers and release state.
- **Commercial clarity** so visitors understand the product without reading internal notes.
