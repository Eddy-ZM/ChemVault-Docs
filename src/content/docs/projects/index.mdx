## Operating principles

- Keep public pages useful without requiring private project context.
- Prefer stable entry routes over scattered one-off links.
- Keep content searchable through descriptive titles and headings.
- Record major public changes in the changelog.
- Keep planned work visible through the roadmap when it affects public users.
- Build before publishing after large imports or navigation changes.
